About Me

Howdy, I'm Davey

Blue Collar Comedian Davey Wester brings a unique presence to today's comedy scene as a Nationwide Headliner. Davey was passed by Mitzi Shore herself and has been a regular at The World Famous Comedy Store in Hollywood, CA for nearly two decades!

When I'm not on stage, you can find me in the great outdoors doing anything that keeps me away from decent WiFi and close to nature. Come catch a show - I promise you'll laugh harder than the time my uncle tried to teach his goat to play fetch.
